Explanations and guides for pure mathematicians

Many guides online are following the engineer principal , i.e. "to teach what is needed to solve the problem". This will make an useful and efficient guide for most of the time. But I have never been satisified by such guide. I am just too used to the pure mathematics way of teaching, namely building step by step from the underlying structure.

This series of blogs is to provide explanation and guides of complicated real world concepts in a more logical and comprehensive manner. It will not be the most suitable for solving a problem, but it will be precise and prograssive. I hope it can help anyone like me
